WHO WE ARE

SPACELAB are a multicultural team of diversely-skilled design professionals, qualified across a wide range of disciplines and technical skills. Our studio in Turner overlooks the light rail corridor on Northbourne Avenue just a short walk from a good coffee in Braddon. Our practice nurtures the energy and enthusiasm of the best urban designers and statutory planners, landscape architects, industrial designers and 3D spatial designers.

Our experience spans decades. Our focus is on the future.

Our Principles

  • Create simple, elegant, enduring design.
  • Create a contemporary aesthetic whilst being respectful of heritage architecture.
  • Pay attention to detail from the large to the small scale.
  • Use natural and contemporary materials.
